The Beginning and the End

The Rail Trail, May 18, 2018

Picture of a trail marker

The Rail Trail that begins here in Saranac and I believe runs to St. Johns is where I have been training for my half marathon. This specific marker is where I start every long run day. Each marker after that is 1 mile exactly. The 131 marker is where I start, and then after running three or four markers towards Ionia, then I turn around and end at this same marker. I am very thankful to have such a flat and well marked area to run in Saranac!
